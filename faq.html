Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using HomeFinder, listing properties, and more. Our goal is to make your experience seamless and stress-free.

Listing a property is easy! Navigate to the "Become a Host" page, sign up or log in, and follow our guided process to input your property details, upload high-quality photos, and set your pricing. Our team reviews all listings for quality and authenticity to ensure trust for all users.

Use the search bar on our homepage or click the "Search" link in the menu. You can filter by location, property type (apartments, homes, or land), price range, and amenities. Save your favorite listings to your account for easy access later.

HomeFinder operates in over 50 countries, including Nigeria, the US, UK, Canada, and more. Contact our support team via the Contact page to confirm availability in your region or to request expansion to new areas.

We use a rigorous verification process, including document checks, property ownership validation, and manual reviews by our team. This ensures all listings are accurate and trustworthy, providing a safe experience for buyers and renters.

HomeFinder charges a small commission per booking or sale, which varies by region and property type. There are no upfront listing fees, and hosts can manage pricing directly. Contact our support team for detailed fee information in your area.

Payments are processed securely through our platform using trusted payment gateways. Buyers and renters pay directly on HomeFinder, and hosts receive payouts after verification, minus our commission. We support multiple payment methods, including credit cards and bank transfers.

Hosts receive 24/7 support via phone, email, or our Contact page, plus access to a host dashboard for managing listings. We provide tips for optimizing listings, pricing guidance, and dedicated assistance for any issues.

Yes, once you’re signed in, you can message hosts directly through our secure messaging system (accessible via the "Messages" link). This ensures safe and private communication without sharing personal contact details.

If you suspect a listing is fraudulent, use the "Report" button on the listing page or contact our support team via the Contact page. We investigate all reports promptly to maintain a safe platform.
